Cell Death and Lysosomes Group

Department of Experimental Medical Science, Lund University, Sweden

Our group studies the molecular regulation of cell death and lysosomes and their role in various diseases (especially neurodegenerative diseases, lysosomal storage disorders, stroke and cancer) with the aim of finding new treatments. For this, we combine basic cell biology, model organism work, clinical aspects, bioimaging, structural biology and different forms of computational science.

 

We are located at the Biomedical Centre (BMC) at Lund University in Sweden, which lies in the vibrant Copenhagen-Malmö region.
